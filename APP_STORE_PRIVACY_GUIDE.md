## üö® COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### ‚ùå DON'T:
1. **Mark photos/memory text as "Linked to User"**
   - These are sent to Google Gemini WITHOUT user ID
   - Mark as "Not Linked to User"

2. **Say you collect Device ID**
   - You do NOT collect IDFA, IDFV, or other tracking identifiers
   - Only collect User ID (from Supabase auth)

3. **Mark any data as "Used for Tracking"**
   - This would trigger ATT prompt
   - You genuinely don't track users

4. **Forget to list third-party SDKs**
   - Apple can detect SDKs in your binary
   - Be transparent about Supabase, RevenueCat, Google Gemini

5. **Use generic privacy policy**
   - Your policy must be specific to MemorySparks
   - Include all third parties and data types
